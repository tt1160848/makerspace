Design Innovation Summer Award (DISA)

Design Innovation Summer Award is a summer Internship for UG students who would like to engage in product design and innovation activities addressing specific needs of industry/people/society. Typical projects supported under DIC for 2016 include: 1. Affordable prosthetic arm with moving parts for amputees 2. Portable directional stereo speakers 3. A prototype developed for polarised imaging based heart rate and blood oxygen saturation measurement 4. Harnessing energy and improving efficiency 5. Indoor navigation system for visually challenged 6. Blood pressure monitoring device 7. Smartphone screen projector 8. Paper recycling: developing a compact machine and associated chemical processes.
