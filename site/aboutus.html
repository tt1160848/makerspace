What is Makerspace IITD?

Makerspace IITD is a part of Design Innovation Centre (DIC) IIT Delhi, which is funded and supported by Ministry of Human Resource and Development (MHRD), Government of India. It is an incentive which provides students physical space and access to equipment and guidance in a flexible format that responds to the interests and skill levels of users collaborating on self-directed projects. It helps educating students in STEM (science, technology, engineering, and mathematics) subjects which fosters digital and information literacy.

Here, at Makerspace IITD, students can avail opportunities to work with technology, bring ideas to motion and, interact with fellow students and experts in new ways, and learn to solve problems, as well as, providing an environment in which they can make mistakes and learn from them. We believe that our makerspace may hold the key to unlocking the potential for innovation in our students and our economy!

What is our motive?

The following aspects, collaboration, exploration, creative thinking and authentic problem solving regardless of the location or design, are a key determiner in deeper learning, and it doesn't take much to see how spaces like these can fit into a variety of educational settings. Working in small groups enhances academic achievements. Giving life to your ideas and see where it takes you. Cooperation promotes higher quality individual problem solving than does competition. Active learning leads to better student attitudes and a significant improvement in thinking and writing. Working in group improves learning outcomes relative to individual work..
